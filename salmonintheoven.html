salmon in the oven

Ingredients

  • 2 1 fillet of salmon, skinned (tails are cheapest) Any veggies you like: tomatoe, peppers, zucchinis, etc Liquid cream Black pepper Salt

    • Method

  • 1. Put the salmon in an oven dish add chopped veggies and cream on top with salt and pepper

  • Grill on a high heat for about 25 min. If it is a thick fillet, it will take longer to cook through, always check if the middle if cooked.

  • You can serve it with rice or pasta
